Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Laxoshedu Quiz Platform free to use?+
Yes! Our core mission is to provide accessible educational tools without hidden fees. You can create unlimited quizzes, share them with anyone, and practice as much as you want—all completely free. We believe quality education should be available to everyone.
Can I use this platform on my mobile phone?+
Absolutely! The entire platform is built with a "Mobile-First" design philosophy. Whether you're using a smartphone, tablet, or desktop computer, Laxoshedu adapts perfectly to your screen size, ensuring a smooth and intuitive experience on any device.
How do I share my quiz with others?+
Once you finish creating a quiz, a unique URL is automatically generated. You can copy and share this link anywhere—via email, social media, messaging apps, or embed it on your website. Anyone with the link can access and take your quiz instantly.
Do I need to create an account to make quizzes?+
No account is required to get started! You can create and share quizzes immediately. However, creating an account allows you to save your quizzes, track responses, and access advanced analytics features. It's completely optional based on your needs.
What types of questions can I create?+
Our platform supports multiple question formats including multiple-choice, true/false, short answer, and fill-in-the-blank questions. You can mix and match different question types in a single quiz to create diverse and engaging assessments.
Can I see who took my quiz and their scores?+
Yes! With a free account, you can track quiz completion, view individual scores, and analyze performance metrics. This helps you understand which topics need more attention and how well your students or audience are grasping the material.
